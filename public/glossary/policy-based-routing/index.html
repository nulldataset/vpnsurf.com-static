Policy-Based Routing

Policy Routing selects routes based on rules (app, IP, domain); it’s often used for Split Tunneling and corporate controls. If a feature sounds vague in marketing, look for measurable signals: audits, documentation, and testable settings. If you’re tuning for speed, test different protocols and nearby servers at the same time of day to account for congestion. When in doubt, prefer simpler configurations: fewer exceptions, fewer custom routes, and verified leak-test results. VPN Surf and Elite VPN guidance aims to keep the concept actionable, not just theoretical.
