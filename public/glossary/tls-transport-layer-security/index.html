TLS (Transport Layer Security)

TLS powers HTTPS; it encrypts web traffic content, while a VPN encrypts at the network layer for many apps. If you travel often, this term becomes especially important because airports, hotels, and cafes are common interception points. For business users, clear policy and least-privilege access reduce risk more than ‘always route everything everywhere.’ For most people, the biggest privacy win is shielding activity from local network observers and reducing ISP-level profiling. VPN Surf and Elite VPN guidance aims to keep the concept actionable, not just theoretical.
